SUSPENSION CAUSES OF THE REGISTER OF IMPORTERS

General Rules of Foreign Trade to 2018

Title 1. General Dispositions and Previous Acts to Dispatch
Chapter 1.3 Register of Importers and Exporters
Rule 1.3.3. Suspension causes in the registers
To the effects of articles 59, fraction IV, of the Law, 84 and 87, last paragraph of the Regulation will proceed the Register of importers suspension and if the Register of Importers of Specific Sectors or the Sectoral Register of Exporters, when:

I. The Taxpayer doesn’t have current and valid e.sign.

II. The importers don’t have registered the e-mail to the effects of the fiscal mailbox.

III. If the taxpayer hasn’t presented the declarations of federal taxes or have complied any other fiscal obligation.

IV. If the taxpayers do not carry out foreign trade operations in a period over 12 months.

V. The taxpayer presents an RFC suspension of activities notice.

VI. The taxpayer presents an RFC cancelation notice.

VII. The taxpayer does not register in the RFC the establishments in which carry its foreign trade operations out.

VIII. The taxpayer makes a change of fiscal domicile or make the change after the start of verification faculties
without concern to the ADSC in accordance to the stablished terms in article 27 of the Federal Fiscal Code.

IX. The taxpayer is not located in its fiscal domicile or the fiscal domicile of the taxpayer or of its establishments are in the inexistent location supposal.

X. The name, denominations or company name or address of the supplier abroad or fiscal domicile of the importer, marked on the entry form, invoice or equivalent document presented and transmitted in accordance of articles 36-A, 37-A and 59-A, of the Law, are fake or inexistent or when the address indicated on those documents is not able to locate the supplier abroad, buyer or receiver abroad.

XI. The taxpayer presents fake documentation.

XII. The taxpayer doesn’t count with the documentation that protects its foreign trade operations as the preview in the applicable legal dispositions.

XIII. The taxpayer modifies the registrations or documents that protect its foreign trade operations.

XIV. The taxpayer does not carry out the accountant, registers, inventories or control media which is mandatory in accordance to tax and customs dispositions; or are hidden modified or are partially or totally destroyed.

XV. The companies that have the Certifications of companies Scheme Register, modality of VAT and IEPS in accordance to rules 7.1.2 and 7.1.3 just as the taxpayers that guarantee the tax interest through deposit or credit letter in accordance to the rule 7.4.1, stop transmitting electronically in accordance to Annex 31 through “Procedures Gate of the SAT”, the information referred in rules 7.2.1, second paragraph, fraction IV and 7.4.3, fraction II in the term stablished to this effect.

XVI. On the entry form is not declared the brand of the imported goods or the information referred on rule 3.1.17.

XVII. A taxpayer registered on the Register of importers or in the Register of importers of Specific Sectors, allows another one that finds itself suspended, keep performing their foreign trade operations, the taxpayer has a legal representative a partner or as a shareholder a member of a company or natural person that has been suspended for any of the stablished causes in this rule and hadn’t detract from.

XVIII. The taxpayer has firm or determined tax credits that, being demandable, are not paid or granted in any of the allowed forms by the CFF, by diverse infractions that the ones indicated on the fraction XIX of this rule and in each are over $100,000.00.

XIX. Through a resolution that determine that a firm tax credit and or demandable in charge of the taxpayer by commission of any of the previewed infractions in articles 176, 177, 179 and 182, fraction II, of the Law, omitting the taxes and compensatory duties over $100,000.00 and said omission represents over the 10% of the total amount that should be paid and the debt being demandable, is not granted.

XX. Through resolution that determines that the declared value on the import entry form is lower by 50% or more to the average price to those identical or similar merchandise imported within a period of 90 days before or after to the operation date.

XXI. The individuals that are subject to criminal proceedings for the alleged commission of crimes in tax matters, industrial property and copyright.

XXII. The amount of income r the value of acts or declared activities in the revised period by the authority is less than the 30% of the value of the imports carried out during the same period.

XXIII. The taxpayer breaches any of the dispositions stablished in the used vehicle Decree in accordance to the stablished in article 9 of the said Decree.

XXIV. The custom authorities have the acknowledgement of the detection by competent authorities, merchandise that attack against industrial property or copyright protected by the Law of the Industrial Property and Copyright Federal Law.

XXV. In the case of definitive export or return abroad of merchandise, is detected that said merchandise didn’t get out of the country or is determined that didn’t carried the return to at least 90% of the merchandise declared of the custom documentation.

XXVI. The taxpayer enters into the fiscal deposit regime in general deposit warehouse authorized in accordance to article 119 of the Law, some of the merchandise referred in rule 4.5.9.

XXVII. The taxpayer starts an operation of internal or international transit and does not make the operation closing to the corresponding destined custom, within the stablished terms, with non-existent justified causes to not perform it.

XXVIII. The taxpayer opposes to the exercise of checking faculties of the custom authorities.

XXIX. The taxpayer, within the checking faculties contemplated in article of the CFF does not attend to the tax or custom authorities’ requirements to present the data and documentation that accredits the compliance of its obligations or make it incompletely.
In the case of different requirements indicated in the previous paragraph, the suspension will proceed when breach more than one occasion with the same requirement.

XXX. On the occasion of the laboratory opinion or the exercise of the checking faculties, the custom authority determines a tariff classification different than the agent, custom representative, importer or exporter have declared in the entry form, when the inaccurate tariff classification implies the non-compliance of any regulation and non-tariff restriction in matters of national security or public health, or the omission of the payment of compensatory duties, as long as the tariff determined by the authority is subject to compensatory duties, when is originated from the origin country declared in the entry form or tariff determined by the custom authority is one of the indicated in Annex A of the Resolution issuing the official forms of the notices and reports that must be submitted by those carrying out vulnerable activities, published in the DOF on the
August 30th, 2013 and the merchandise is located in annotation of article 17, fraction XIV, of the LFPIORPI

XXXI. On the occasion of custom recognition or exercise of the checking faculties, the customs authority detects goods that physically bear or mark of origin which corresponds to a country that export goods under conditions of unfair international trade practices, are subject to the payment of a compensatory duty or a transition measure, and the origin declared in the motion is different.

XXXII. On the occasion of custom recognition or merchandise in transport verification, the custom authority detects the introduction to national territory weapons or narcotics indicated in article 193 of the CPF, without the documentation that accredits the compliance of regulations or corresponding non-tariff restrictions or if is prohibited merchandise.

XXXIII. On the occasion of custom recognition or merchandise in transport verification, is detected to the taxpayer by the custom authority, quantities in cash, national or foreign checks, payments order or any document receivable or any combination of them, superior to the equivalent to the national currency about 30,000 USD and have omitted the declarations to the custom authority at the moment of going in or out the country.

XXXIV. Is detected that companies with IMMEX program, do not have temporary imported goods under the protection of its program in the registered addresses to the SAT in accordance to article 24, fraction VI of the IMMEX Decree, or does not have with the facilities needed to carry out the maquila operations.

XXXV. The Economy Secretary had cancelled the IMMEX program in accordance to the stablished in article 27 of IMMEX Decree, unless the taxpayer has active another program of sectoral promotion referred in article 7 of the said Decree, or if the registration had been performed in accordance to rule 1.3.2. and after to the obtention of the IMMEX program.

XXXVI. In the case of taxpayers registered in the Sector 2 “Radioactive and Nuclear”, section A from Annex 10, when the National Commission of Nuclear Safety and Safeguards of the SENER, notifies to the ACIA, any of the licenses or authorizations, according the case, indicated in Section “requirements” of the procedure file 6/LA, were suspended or cancelled.

XXXVII. In the case of taxpayers registered in the Sector “Cigarettes”, section A, Annex 10, when the COFEPRIS, notifies to the ACIA, that the sanitary license provided to the importers were suspended, cancelled or revoked or when the registered domiciles by the importers to the COFEPRIS is not the same as the registered in the SAT. Likewise, when the cigarettes branches to import are not classified in the Annex 11 of RMF.

XXXVIII. To the effects of sector 10 “Footwear” and 11 “Textile and Clothing”, section A, Annex 10, when the Import Automated Notices to present to the clearance of goods do not protect the merchandise to import or are not current.

XXXIX. To the effects of sector 10 “Footwear” and 11 “Textile and Clothing”, section A, Annex 10, when is obliged to present custom account of guarantee in accordance to article 86-A, fraction I, of the Law, is omitted its presentation or contains wrong information that represent a less amount that should have guaranteed. In that case preview in article 158, fraction I, of the Law, the suspension of the Register of Importers will proceed if the guarantee is not presented within a term of 15 days referred in the last paragraph of the said article 158.

XL. The taxpayers registered in the sector 13 “Hydrocarbons and Fuels” section A, Annex 10, do not count with the current authorizations referred in the Hydrocarbons Law, published in the DOF in August 11th,2014, likewise those taxpayers perform or had performed operations or hired services with subjects that does not have the respective permissions, in the case of regulated activities in terms of the cited Law, or when the taxpayer does not present, before the first importation of merchandise which request the register in accordance to file 6/LA, copy of the contracts and in the case of imports by ducts from the “Authorization for the introduction or extraction of merchandise from the national territory, by pipelines, cables or other means capable of conduction” issued by the ACAJA, in terms of article 39 of the Regulations, rule 2.4.3 and procedure file 35/LA.

XLI. To the effects of sector 8 “Iron ores and their concentrates” section B, Annex 10, when the documents, authorizations or permission are incomplete, present inconsistencies or are not valid.

XLII. The taxpayers register on sector 9 “Gold, silver and bronze” section B, annex 10, that export classified goods in tariffs 7404.00.01, 7404.00.02 and 7404.00.99, does not comply with the stablished in the subsection d) number 4, item “Requirements” from section A of the “instructive of procedure of the Registration Authorization for the sector exporters sector (rule 1.3.7) of the “Authorization of the register to the sectorial exporter (rule 1.3.7)”.
